Our Beef

At our farm, we take pride in raising the highest quality beef for our customers. Our cattle are grass-fed and finished, which means they are never given hormones or antibiotics and are free to roam on pasture. Here are some of the reasons why our beef is the best:

  • Healthier: Grass-fed beef is leaner and has less saturated fat than conventionally raised beef.
  • Tastier: Grass-fed beef has a more complex and rich flavor than conventionally raised beef.
  • Environmentally friendly: Grass-fed beef is better for the environment because it promotes soil health and reduces greenhouse gas emissions.
  • Humanely raised: Our cattle are raised in a stress-free environment and are treated with care and respect.

We offer a variety of cuts of beef, including:

  • Steaks: Our steaks are aged for maximum flavor and tenderness.
  • Ground beef: Our ground beef is made from our own grass-fed beef and is perfect for burgers, meatballs, and other recipes.
  • Ribs: Our ribs are fall-off-the-bone tender and packed with flavor.
  • Roasts: Our roasts are perfect for slow-cooking and are a delicious and easy meal option.
